Sneaker Heads

The Jordan 1 High debuted in 1985 as the first
signature sneaker developed by Nike for Michael Jordan.
The Peter Moore designed performance basketball
sneaker featured a simple Nike Dunk inspired design that
incorporated the Nike Swoosh and the Jordan Wings logo
and featured Nike Air.

Michael Jordan's second signature model, the Jordan 2 first debuted in November 1986, and was designed by Bruce Killgore/Peter Moore. Created with high-end luxury in mind, the AJ2 was notable for its rich leather upper, lizard skin-style detailing, and noticeable lack of a Swoosh
